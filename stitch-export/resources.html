Frequently Asked Questions

Need help navigating the library? Start here.

Are all resources free to download? expand_more
Yes, as part of the Ascend Mentorship Program, all resources in this library are completely free for registered mentors and mentees. We believe in providing equal access to tools for success.
How often is the library updated? expand_more
We add new guides, templates, and insights on a monthly basis. Be sure to check the "Featured Resource" banner for the latest major additions, and look out for our monthly newsletter roundups.
Can I submit my own resources? expand_more
Absolutely! We encourage community contributions. If you have a template or guide that has worked well for your mentorship journey, please contact our support team. Submissions are reviewed quarterly.
